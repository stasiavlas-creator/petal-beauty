From shimmery oils to glass-skin lotions — the body care Pinterest is obsessing over this season.

The Layering Method

The Pinterest approach to body glow is a layered ritual, not a single product. Start with Nécessaire The Body Lotion on clean, slightly damp skin for a deep hydration base. Then layer either the Sol de Janeiro Glowmotions or the Nuxe Huile Prodigieuse Or on top for luminosity — the first for a bronzed, sun-kissed finish, the second for a softer golden glow. On non-shimmer days, swap to the OUAI Body Crème or the Glow Recipe Pink Dream Cream for a glazed, glass-skin effect.

The result is the kind of glowing, hydrated skin that summer demands — and that Pinterest has been curating all year.
