Best Hair Growth Oils
Pinterest Loves Right Now

Rosemary, honey, and bond-repair formulas — the scalp and hair oils transforming routines in 2026.

Hair oiling is no longer an obscure Ayurvedic practice — it is the most-saved hair ritual on Pinterest in 2026. From scalp stimulation with rosemary to bond repair with peptide-infused formulas, the right oil can transform your hair health over weeks. We dug into the data to find which oils are dominating boards right now.

Why Hair Oiling Works

Regular oil application nourishes the scalp, reduces protein loss, and protects strands from heat and environmental damage. The key is choosing the right oil for your specific hair goal — growth, shine, frizz control, or damage repair. The six products below cover every need, as evidenced by their collective millions of Pinterest saves.

How to Build a Routine

The most effective Pinterest hair routines layer these products strategically. Start with scalp oiling (Mielle Rosemary) two to three nights per week. On wash days, follow with the K18 treatment before any styling. Then apply the OUAI leave-in as a base, Olaplex No.7 for heat protection, and finish with Gisou or Bumble's invisible oil for shine. The difference in hair texture within four to six weeks is dramatic.
