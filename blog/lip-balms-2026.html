Top 10 Lip Balms
Trending on Pinterest 2026

From sheer butter balms to peptide-packed treatments — the lip products every board is saving right now.

Lip care has had a serious moment on Pinterest. The days of a single drugstore ChapStick are long gone — boards are overflowing with glazed, glossy, plump-looking lips achieved through next-generation balms, oils, and overnight treatments. We analysed the most-saved lip products across millions of Pinterest boards and compiled the definitive list for 2026.

The Full List

No. 1
Summer Fridays Lip Butter Balm
Summer Fridays

This is the lip balm that broke the internet — and it earns every pin. The whipped, butter-soft texture applies like a dream, delivering instant moisture and a sheer, glossy finish that lasts for hours. Available in multiple sheer-tinted shades, it sits at the intersection of skincare and lip gloss.

No. 2
LANEIGE Lip Sleeping Mask Berry
LANEIGE

A genuine cult classic. The Lip Sleeping Mask has dominated overnight lip care for years, and 2026 is no different. Apply a generous layer before bed and wake up to pillowy soft, smooth lips. The berry scent and candy-pink tub have made it as pinnable as it is effective.

No. 3
Rhode Peptide Lip Treatment
Rhode

Hailey Bieber's skincare brand launched with this peptide lip treatment and it immediately took over Pinterest. The formula is rich but not sticky, delivering peptides and conditioning ingredients for a plump, glossy pout. No. 4
Laneige Lip Glowy Balm

The daytime companion to the Sleeping Mask. This sheer tinted balm with shea butter gives lips a glossy, hydrated look without any stickiness. The Gummy Bear shade — a soft translucent pink — is consistently one of the most-pinned lip products of the year.

Summer Fridays Dream Lip Oil
Summer Fridays

Summer Fridays proves they own the lip care category with this nourishing lip oil. Lightweight yet conditioning, it delivers a glossy sheer tint that enhances your natural lip colour. Layer it over the Lip Butter Balm for an unstoppable combination that Pinterest users swear by.

Charlotte Tilbury Pillow Talk Lip Liner
Charlotte Tilbury

Pillow Talk is the universal lip shade that works on virtually every skin tone, and the liner version remains a perennial Pinterest favourite. The Verdict

The common thread across all these products is that they prioritise hydration, glossiness, and a natural-looking finish. The heavy, matte lip trend has firmly given way to plump, glazed, well-nourished lips — and these are the products making that look possible. Whether you invest in the Rhode Peptide Lip Treatment or reach for the budget-friendly Laneige balm, you cannot go wrong.

The most important thing? Consistency. Apply a hydrating balm morning and night, treat with an overnight mask a few times a week, and your lips will thank you.
