What is PETAL?

Beauty discovery,
Pinterest-powered

PETAL is a curation platform built for the Pinterest generation. We analyse thousands of beauty boards, viral pins, and trending saves to surface the products people are genuinely excited about — not what's advertised, but what's being organically shared and loved.

We believe the most powerful recommendations come from real people sharing what actually works. Pinterest is the world's largest beauty mood board — and PETAL is your guide to everything on it.

How it works

Trend tracking,
done for you

Our team monitors Pinterest weekly, tracking which beauty products are gaining momentum across boards, saves, and repins. When a product emerges as a genuine trend — not a flash in the pan — we add it to our catalogue with honest descriptions, Amazon star ratings, and real review counts.

We never sell products directly. Every item on PETAL links straight to Amazon — trusted fulfilment, fast Prime shipping, and easy returns. We earn a small affiliate commission when you buy, but this never influences what we feature. Our curation is driven entirely by Pinterest data.
